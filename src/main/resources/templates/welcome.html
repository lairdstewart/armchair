About

Armchair is Beli for books. You get two lists: fiction and non-fiction. As you add books you'll rank them head-to-head. Rank 10 books to receive personalized recommendations. To publish your lists, visit the Profile tab.

Privacy

Anyone can view public profiles and curated lists. To create a list, you'll need to sign up. User authentication is through Google or GitHub. Armchair is not given your email, profile picture, or account data. Profiles are private by default. The code is open source.
