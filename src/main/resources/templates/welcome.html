About

Armchair is Beli for books. You get two lists: fiction and non-fiction. As you add books you'll rank them head-to-head. Like Beli, this provides a better signal of true book preferences and enables better recommendations.

Privacy

Anyone can make lists. To save your lists, you'll need to sign up. Only Google authentication is supported at the moment. Armchair is not given your email or profile, only a unique identifier provided by Google. Google, of course, can see that you've visited the page, but that's better than us managing your password. All profiles are public and visible in Search Profiles.
