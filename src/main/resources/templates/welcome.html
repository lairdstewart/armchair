About

Armchair is Beli for books. Rank the books you've read head-to-head, and share your lists with friends. Discover new books from other users and our curated lists. Rank 10 books to receive personalized recommendations.

Privacy

Anyone can view profiles and curated lists. To create a list, you'll need to sign up via Google, Microsoft, or GitHub. Armchair is not given your email, profile picture, or account data. All profiles are public. The code is open source.

Support

Report bugs or send suggestions to help@armchairlist.com.
